1. The Concept

The Lucky Wheel is a game of chance where players place their bets on specific segments and spin the wheel to try to win rewards.

2. How to Play

  • Choose a bet amount
  • Select a segment of the wheel
  • Spin the wheel and wait for the result

3. Strategies

  • Bet on segments with high payouts for potential massive winnings
  • Play conservatively by betting on multiple segments
  • Manage your bankroll to maximize your playtime

4. Winning and Losing

  • If the wheel stops on your segment, you win the corresponding amount
  • If it stops elsewhere, you lose your bet
  • Bonus spins can be activated depending on the game
