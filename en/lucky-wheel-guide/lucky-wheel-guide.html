Set your commitment

Choose your application with the intuitive slider or input field. The minimum insert is 1.00, the maximum stone set 1,000.00.

  • Use the slider for quick adjustments
  • Enter exact amounts in the input field

Choose the multiplier

Determine your target multiplier. Higher multipliers mean larger profits, but a lower probability of winning.

  • 2x - 5x: Higher chances of winning, less reward
  • 10x - 20x: Balanced risk/reward
  • 100x: High risk, maximum profit

Spin the wheel

Click on the spin button and watch the bike decide your fate! The pointer ends up on your multiplier when you win.

  • Auto-spin available for continuous games
  • Auto-spin can be stopped at any time
  • Pay attention to the multiplier-revealing effect

Collect profits

If you win, your use will be multiplied by the landed multiplier. Profits are automatically added to your credit.

  • Detailed profit history available

Professional tips

Start up

Start with lower multipliers to understand game mechanics. Practice with 2x-5x before you take greater risks.

Know when it is

Set profit and loss limits before you play. Hold on your limits, no matter how the game goes out to play responsibly.

Develop a strategy

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

What is the payout rate (RTP)?

WaifuSpin has an RTP of 99%. This means that players receive an average of €99 for each €100.

Can I stop a spin?

Spins started once cannot be canceled. Make sure you confirm your use and multiplier before turning.

Is there a profit limit?

Yes, the maximum profit is limited to 100 times your use. This means that you can win up to €100,000 with a maximum of €1,000.

How does Auto-Spin work?

Auto-spin automatically turns the bike on with your selected use and multiplier. You can set a limitation for the number of spins or a loss limit.
