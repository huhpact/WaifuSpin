Basics of the slot machines

Random principle (RNG)

Modern slot machines use complex algorithms to generate random numbers. These RNG systems guarantee fair and unpredictable results for every spin.

Fairness

Payout rate (RTP)

The RTP (return to player) is typically between 96% and 99%. An RTP of 99% means that theoretically €99 of €100 use is paid back to players.

Average

Volatility

The volatility determines the risk-profit ratio. High volatility means rarer but higher profits, low volatility more frequent but smaller profits.

Strategies & style of play

1. Budgetmanagement

  • Set a fixed daily budget
  • Share the budget into game units
  • Use Stopless and Take Profit Limits

2. Game selection

  • Check the RTP values
  • Choose suitable volatility
  • Note bonus features
Importance: 85%

Important terms

Free spins

Free spins that are often triggered by special symbols. Additional multipliers or features can be active during the free spins.

Frequency

Scatter

Special symbols that can activate bonus features regardless of paylines. Usually 3 or more scatter are required for triggering.

Value

Wild-Symbol

Replaces other symbols for better winning combinations. Special variants such as Expanding Wilds or Sticky Wilds offer additional chances of winning.
