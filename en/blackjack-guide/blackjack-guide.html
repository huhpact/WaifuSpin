Blackjack Master

Learn the classic casino card game step by step

Scroll to learn

1. The Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. Face cards are worth 10, aces are worth 1 or 11, and all other cards retain their numeric value.

2. The Deal

Each player is dealt two face-up cards. The dealer receives one face-up card and one face-down card (the hole card). Players must decide whether to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current cards).

3. Player Decisions

After receiving your initial cards, you can:

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet and receive one additional card
  • Split: If you have a pair, split it into two separate hands

4. Winning and Losing

You win if:

  • Your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's
  • The dealer busts (exceeds 21)
  • You get a natural Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card)
