What is Limbo?

Limbo is a demonstrably fair casino game in which the players bet on whether a randomly generated multiplier exceeds his chosen destination. The higher your goal, the greater your potential winnings.

How it works

The game creates a multiplier between 1.00× and possibly infinitely infinite. You win when the multiplier is higher than your prediction. Lower predictions are easier to win, but offer smaller payments.
