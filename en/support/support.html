How can we help you?

How can I deposit cryptocurrencies?
To deposit cryptocurrencies, go to your wallet section and select the cryptocurrency of your choice. You will receive a unique deposit address. Send your crypto to this address and it will be credited to your account after network confirmation.
What cryptocurrencies are accepted?
We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and various other popular cryptocurrencies. Check our deposit page for the full list of supported coins.
How do I enable 2FA security?
Go to your account settings, select "Security" and click "Enable 2FA". Scan the QR code with your authentication app and enter the code to confirm. We highly recommend enabling this feature.
What are the withdrawal times?
Withdrawals are processed automatically and usually take 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the cryptocurrency network congestion. VIP members enjoy priority processing.
How can I join the VIP program?
Our VIP program is invitation-based and considers your gaming history, deposit volume, and account activity. Reach higher levels naturally by playing your favorite games!
